ABOUT THE CAFE

The local cafe is a charming spot in downtown, popular among locals and tourists. Rustic decor, fresh coffee, and homemade treats make for a cozy and inviting atmosphere. The friendly staff and outdoor seating add to the experience. Art exhibits and live music performances make the cafe a vibrant and eclectic destination.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Richard Dexter

Opening hours: everyday from 9am to 9pm.

Address: 12 Avenue Bangalore-69
